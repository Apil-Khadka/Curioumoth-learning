Curious Moths

Curious Moths is a group of individuals who are passionate about exploring the world and learning new things. The group was founded with the goal of creating a welcoming and supportive community for people who share a curiosity about the mysteries of the world.

Members of Curious Moths come from all walks of life and have a wide range of interests. The group regularly holds events and discussions on a variety of topics, and encourages members to share their knowledge and experiences with each other. In addition to hosting events and discussions, Curious Moths also organizes outings and trips to museums, exhibitions, and other educational venues.

Curious Moths is a diverse and inclusive community that is open to people of all ages and backgrounds. If you are interested in joining the group, you can find more information on the group's website or by contacting the organizers. We hope to see you at one of our events soon!
